Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions about using the Ikenne LGA Portal answered.

How do I make a digital tax payment?

To make a digital tax payment, first register or log in to the Ikenne LGA Portal. Navigate to the Tax Payment section, select your tax category (e.g., property, business, or agricultural), and follow the step-by-step instructions to complete the payment securely through our integrated gateway.

How do I apply for a permit or license?

Visit the Permit Applications section on the portal. You will need to fill out a digital application form, upload the required documents, and submit your application. You can track the status of your application directly within your dashboard.

How does the E-Agro Marketplace work?

The E-Agro Marketplace allows local farmers and vendors to list their products online. Buyers can browse available items, place orders, and make payments securely via the portal. Order tracking and vendor ratings help ensure a transparent marketplace environment.

What are the steps for digital business registration?

To register your business, navigate to the Digital Business Registration section. Fill in the guided forms with your business details and valid documentation. Once submitted, your application will be verified, and you will receive your registration confirmation along with automated tax tracking.

How can I request an official certificate?

The Certificate Issuance feature lets you request official documents such as birth, marriage, and death certificates. Simply choose the certificate type, complete the online request form, and download the certificate once your request is approved.

What is Tele-Medicine and how do I access it?

Tele-Medicine is a service that allows you to schedule remote medical consultations with qualified healthcare professionals. To access it, go to the Tele-Medicine section on the portal, book an appointment, and consult with a doctor via video call.

How do I access my digital health records?

Once you register for our E-Healthcare services, you can securely log in to your personal dashboard to view and update your health records. Our platform guarantees high-level security to protect your medical information.
